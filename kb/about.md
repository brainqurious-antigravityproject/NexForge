# About Sitecraf

## Who We Are

Sitecraf is a web studio based in South Ex, New Delhi. We work remotely with clients across India and internationally. We specialize in building fast, modern websites using WordPress, Wix Studio, Shopify, Next.js, and custom web applications.

## Who We Work With

We partner with:
- Small businesses and service providers looking to establish an online presence
- D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) brands and e-commerce startups
- Local businesses wanting to reach more customers digitally
- Agencies needing development support for their clients
- Startups building custom web applications and platforms

## Why Choose Sitecraf

**Fixed Written Quote**: We provide a detailed, written quote before any work begins. No surprises, no scope creep.

**Transparent Payment Structure**: 50% payment upfront after you approve the design mockup, 50% on launch. No balance negotiation after delivery.

**Complete IP Ownership**: 100% intellectual property ownership is transferred to you. You own the code, design, and content. No vendor lock-in.

**No Hidden Costs**: No GST markup, no lock-in contracts, no mandatory support purchases. Pay only for what you use.

## Trust and Transparency

**No Sales Calls Required**: We gather requirements through a structured onboarding questionnaire. No awkward sales calls—just clear, written communication.

**Written Timelines**: Every project has a documented timeline with clear milestones. You'll know exactly when each phase completes.

**Post-Launch Support**: 2 weeks of free technical support after your website launches. Hosting is billed separately.

**Structured Onboarding**: We use a detailed questionnaire to understand your business, target audience, and goals before design begins.
